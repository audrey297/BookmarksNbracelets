The Story Behind the Shop

A one-woman shop
built on books
& bracelets.

Hi — I'm the whole team. BookmarksNbracelets started because I couldn't find a gift that felt personal enough for the readers in my life. So I made one. Then another. Then someone asked if they could buy one.

Now I sit down with beads, thread, leather, and a lot of intention and make things that I'd genuinely want to keep for myself. Every order is made fresh — I don't keep a stockroom, I keep a craft table.

"Every bookmark holds a story. Every bracelet holds a memory. I try to make both worth keeping."
